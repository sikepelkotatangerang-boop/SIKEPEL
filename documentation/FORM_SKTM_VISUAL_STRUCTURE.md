## ğŸ¨ Color Scheme

### Card Headers
- **Icon Background**: `bg-yellow-500` (SKTM), customize per form
- **Icon Color**: `text-white`
- **Icon Size**: `w-6 h-6` (header), `w-5 h-5` (section)
- **Text Color**: `text-primary-600`

### Alerts
- **Error**: `bg-red-50 border-red-200 text-red-800`
- **Warning**: `bg-yellow-50 border-yellow-200 text-yellow-800`
- **Info**: `bg-blue-50 border-blue-200 text-blue-700`
- **Success**: `bg-green-50 border-green-200 text-green-800`

### Form Elements
- **Border**: `border-gray-300`
- **Focus Ring**: `focus:ring-2 focus:ring-primary-500`
- **Disabled**: `bg-gray-50 cursor-not-allowed`
- **Required**: `text-red-500` (asterisk)

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Default Button style
- **Outline**: `variant="outline"`
- **Disabled**: `opacity-50 cursor-not-allowed`
